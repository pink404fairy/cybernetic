About Cybernetics Auto

Based in Nairobi, Kenya, Cybernetics Auto Limited Company is dedicated to transforming the educational landscape by providing cutting-edge technology solutions. Our focus is on enhancing teaching and learning processes, significantly improving student outcomes, and simplifying administrative tasks for educational institutions.

Our flagship cloud-based School Management System offers unparalleled accessibility and efficiency, allowing access from anywhere, at any time. We equip teachers and administrators with the essential tools and insights needed for effective student progress monitoring and fostering a thriving learning environment, ultimately helping students achieve their maximum potential.

Understanding the financial barriers to education in Kenya, Cybernetics Auto is also committed to providing accessible financial resources. Recognizing that educational costs can be a significant burden for households, we have developed the School Fees Loans Issuance Program, a resourceful solution to ensure students are not turned away from school due to lack of fees.

How Our School Fees Loan Program Works

Getting a school fees loan from Cybernetics Auto is a simple and straightforward process designed to help parents quickly and easily access the funds they need.

Application Process

Provide personal information, income details, student bio, and credit history. Our system performs a quick credit check to assess your eligibility and determine loan terms.

Approval and Agreement

Our system reviews your application and decides on eligibility and loan amount. Upon approval, you receive a loan notification detailing the amount, repayment period, and interest rate. Confirm your commitment to proceed.

Disbursement and Repayment

After your confirmation, the approved loan amount is deposited directly into the school's account. Repayment begins according to the agreed schedule, with options for early payoff or small installments.
